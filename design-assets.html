Starting with wireframing gave me the structure I needed to begin building my website. Although the final design evolved significantly, the wireframe served as a crucial blueprint; it outlined the layout, clarified priorities, and helped me visualize the user experiences.

Brand Story

The brand SHE originates from my own name, making it personal while also standing as an empowering statement for women in tech. This portfolio design reflects a commitment to confidence, clarity, and progress,not only in my own career but also in uplifting and inspiring other women along the way.

I chose a modern blue-based color palette to express trust, calmness, and intelligence,values I believe are essential in creating meaningful, impactful work. The typography is a balance of bold and refined.
